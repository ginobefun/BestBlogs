## 中文

### 🚀 早鸟订阅正式开放

**全新定价，现在升级最划算**
- 早鸟价：**$4.9/月**，2026 年 9 月 1 日前订阅均享此价格
- 早鸟期结束后正式定价为 $9.9/月
- 早鸟价一旦锁定，后续续订长期保留

### ✨ 新功能

**Pro 早报升级为纯图文版**
- Pro 早报现在以图文形式呈现，排版更清晰、加载更快
- 历史已生成的播客版本仍可正常播放，不受影响
- 图文版支持中英文切换，内容与原来完全一致

**连接 X 账号，自动同步你的关注**
- 在设置页绑定 X 账号，BestBlogs 自动把你在 X 上关注的人同步为订阅源
- 之后这些账号的推文会直接出现在你的内容流里，无需手动添加
- 如果你同时是 xgo.ing 用户，BestBlogs Pro 与 xgo.ing Pro 会员权益自动互通

**社区源进入发现页**
- 其他用户提交并经过审核的订阅源，现在会出现在「添加订阅源」的发现页
- 带有「社区源」标识，方便区分
- 你已有的订阅和源列表完全不受影响

**RSS 订阅地址上线**
- 无需账号即可订阅：每日早报 `/rss/brief`、热门内容 `/rss/trending`、主题专栏 `/rss/topic/{slug}`
- 支持任何 RSS 阅读器（Reeder、NetNewsWire、Feedly 等）

**CLI 工具和 Agent Skills 新增命令**
- CLI 新增：获取当日/最新早报、查看热门内容（按日/周/月）、浏览主题专栏列表
- 三个新 Agent Skills：`bestblogs-brief`、`bestblogs-trending`、`bestblogs-topic`

### 🔧 体验优化

**注册后推荐内容更精准**
- 注册时选择的兴趣标签现在会真正影响「为你推荐的订阅源」，不再是全站热门统一推送

**英文版文案全面打磨**
- 约 50 处英文表达改写，去掉了直译和中式表达，统一了常用术语

## English

### 🚀 Early Bird Subscriptions Now Open

**New pricing — best time to subscribe**
- Early bird: **$4.9/month** for any subscription before September 1, 2026
- Regular price after the early bird period: $9.9/month
- Lock in the early bird price now and keep it as long as you stay subscribed

### ✨ New Features

**Pro brief is now text-only**
- The Pro daily brief now comes in a clean text edition — faster to load, easier to skim
- Previously generated podcast episodes remain playable; nothing is removed
- The text edition supports both Chinese and English, with the same content you had before

**Connect X to sync who you follow**
- Go to Settings, bind your X account, and BestBlogs turns the accounts you follow on X into subscriptions automatically
- Their tweets will appear in your content feed — no manual setup needed
- If you also use xgo.ing, BestBlogs Pro and xgo.ing Pro memberships sync automatically

**Community sources in discovery**
- Sources submitted by other users and approved by the platform now appear in the Add Sources discovery page with a "Community" badge
- Your existing subscriptions and source list are not affected

**RSS feeds are live**
- No account needed: subscribe to the daily brief, trending content, or topic columns via any RSS reader
- Works with Reeder, NetNewsWire, Feedly, and any other RSS app

**New CLI commands and Agent Skills**
- CLI adds: fetch today's brief, browse trending content by day/week/month, and list topic columns
- Three new Agent Skills: `bestblogs-brief`, `bestblogs-trending`, `bestblogs-topic`

### 🔧 Improvements

**Better source recommendations after sign-up**
- The interests you pick during onboarding now genuinely shape which sources are recommended — instead of the same popular defaults for everyone

**English copy polish**
- About 50 copy rewrites to remove Chinglish and align common terms across the site
