## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

- Pro 早报全新视觉：评分改为五星 + 数字，日期更醒目，关键词以 hashtag 形式呈现，邮件样式同步焕新

### 🔧 体验优化

- 我的源：在添加更多源里关注后，每页大小、当前页、筛选条件不再丢失，仅看未关注切换更直观
- X 关注：你主动取消的 X 关注，下次同步不会再被恢复

## English

### ✨ New Features

- Refreshed Pro Brief: five-star rating with score, bigger hero date, hashtag keywords, and a matching new email design

### 🔧 Improvements

- My Sources: following from the add view no longer resets your page size, current page, or filters; the Hide Following toggle is clearer
- X Follows: any X follow you cancel manually stays cancelled and is no longer re-added by sync
