## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

**每日回顾正式上线**

每日回顾现已对所有用户开放。AI 每天自动读取你的阅读足迹，从当天质量最高的内容中提炼：

- **核心要点**：当天最值得记住的知识点摘要
- **关键引用**：来自原文的精彩段落与金句
- **思考题**：帮助你深化理解的反思问题

思考题卡片现在出现在足迹列表之前，让你趁洞察新鲜时第一时间看到引导。

回顾内容根据你的语言设置自动生成中文或英文版本。

## English

### ✨ New Features

**Daily Review Now Live**

Daily Review is now available to all users. Every day, AI reads through your reading footprint and distills highlights from your highest-quality content into:

- **Key takeaways**: The most memorable insights from the day's reading
- **Key quotes**: Standout passages and highlights from the original articles
- **Reflection questions**: Prompts to help you deepen your understanding

The reflection questions card now appears before the footprint list, so you see the prompts while the insights are still fresh.

Reviews are automatically generated in your preferred language (Chinese or English) based on your account settings.
