## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

- 「我的关注」新增订阅源库入口：工作台左栏现有「浏览订阅源库」按钮，可按领域和语言筛选平台精选源，勾选后批量关注，关注结果即时同步至订阅列表
- 内容广场即时筛选：点击文章、推文、播客卡片上的分类、标签、语言或来源名，即可快速缩小列表范围；已选条件以标签形式显示，支持单独移除或一键清除
- 划线笔记延伸到更多区域：文章 AI 摘要、视频与播客概要等区域现在也支持划线、写笔记和 AI 提问，各区域划线互不干扰

### 🔧 体验优化

- Pro 早报推荐更贴合兴趣：推荐引擎引入「兴趣预算」机制，按订阅核心内容、探索发现、广度兜底三段比例配比，同一来源文章不再集中出现，早报内容更均衡多样
- 内容广场推文列表体验对齐：推文列表的筛选工具栏与视角切换和文章、播客等类型保持一致，切换内容类型后操作习惯不变
- 关闭公告横幅后不再重复展示：手动关闭通知后，同一条公告不会在下次访问时重新出现
- 品牌图标焕新：网站 Logo 与浏览器 favicon 更新为新版图标

## English

### ✨ New Features

- Source library in My Follow: the workspace sidebar now has a Browse Source Library button where you can filter curated sources by topic and language, select multiple, and follow them in one click — your subscription list updates instantly
- Click-to-filter in the content square: tap any category, tag, language, or source name on a card to narrow the list right away; active filters appear as removable chips so you can adjust or clear them individually
- Highlighting extended to more areas: AI summaries, video overviews, and podcast chapter outlines now support highlighting, notes, and AI questions — each area keeps its own set of highlights without mixing

### 🔧 Improvements

- More personalized Pro brief: the recommendation engine now uses an interest-budget model that allocates content across subscription-core, quality exploration, and discovery slots — so no single source dominates and every brief covers more ground
- Consistent tweet list experience: the tweet tab in the content square now has the same filter toolbar and perspective tabs as articles and podcasts, making it feel seamless to switch between content types
- Announcement banners stay closed: dismissing a notice now remembers your choice, so the same banner will not reappear on your next visit
- Refreshed brand icon: the site logo and browser favicon have been updated to the new design
