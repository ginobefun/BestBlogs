## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

**Pro 用户邮件早报正式上线**
- Pro 用户现在可以在设置页开启每日早报邮件推送，每天早上发送到你的邮箱
- **图文完整版 Newsletter**：Top 3 头条深度摘要（3-5 句核心内容 + 2-3 条关键洞察 + 2-3 篇扩展阅读），以及第 4-10 条精选摘要，适合深度阅读
- **图文精简版 Newsletter**：10 条全部以一两句摘要呈现，通勤或碎片时间快速扫读
- 支持 Gmail / Outlook / Apple Mail 等主流邮件客户端
- 根据你的语言偏好，自动发送中文或英文版本
- 邮件底部一键退订，随时可以关闭

**用户菜单新增快捷入口**
- 头像下拉菜单新增「我的收藏」和「阅读历史」快速入口，不再需要记忆路径

### 🔧 体验优化

**Settings 页面重新组织**
- 「兴趣设置」调整为第一个区块，方便新用户快速完成个性化配置
- 早报格式（图文版 / 精简版 / 播客版）切换后**立即生效**，无需再点保存按钮
- 「播客版」用户同样可以开启邮件推送，发送时自动使用图文版，增强 Pro 用户权益
- 首次开启邮件推送时，弹出提示告知「明天早上起生效」，避免疑惑

**onboarding 引导修复**
- 修复了部分用户反复弹出兴趣引导弹窗的问题
- 重新打开引导时，已选过的标签会自动回填，不需要重新选择

### 🐛 移动端问题修复

- **表格横向滚动**：文章正文中的宽表格可以左右滑动，不再逐字换行
- **iframe 大片空白**：第三方嵌入内容（如视频、演示）被浏览器屏蔽时，现在自动显示「在新标签页打开」链接，不再出现白块
- **播放器遮挡**：修复了移动端底部导航栏和迷你播放器互相遮挡的问题，页面内容不会被压在播放器下方
- **头像菜单视觉抖动**：打开下拉菜单时页面宽度不再跳动
- **页面横向溢出**：修复了在某些页面出现不该有的横向滚动条的问题
- **英文「Not Interested」按钮换行**：在窄屏下现在仅显示图标，宽屏保持完整文字
- **OpenAI 等复杂布局文章**：修复了某些来自 OpenAI 等使用复杂 CSS 布局的文章，在 BestBlogs 内显示错位的问题

## English

### ✨ New Features

**Pro Daily Brief Email Delivery**
- Pro users can now enable Daily Brief email delivery in Settings. Your email arrives every morning (exact time may vary during beta)
- **Full Newsletter**: Top 3 headline items with 3-5 sentence deep summaries, 2-3 key insights, and 2-3 related reads each — plus quick summaries for items 4-10
- **Compact Newsletter**: All 10 items as one-or-two-sentence summaries — ideal for commutes and quick scanning
- Compatible with Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, and all major clients
- Automatically sent in Chinese or English based on your language preference
- One-click unsubscribe at the bottom of every email

**User Menu Quick Links**
- "My Bookmarks" and "Reading History" are now directly accessible from the avatar dropdown menu

### 🔧 Improvements

**Settings Page Reorganization**
- "Interests" is now the first section — easier for new users to personalize immediately
- Brief format changes (Newsletter / Compact / Podcast) **save instantly** without clicking a Save button
- "Podcast" format users can also enable email delivery — emails are sent in Newsletter format, adding an extra Pro benefit
- First-time email opt-in shows a toast: "Your first Newsletter arrives tomorrow morning"

**Onboarding Flow Fix**
- Fixed repeated onboarding dialog appearing for some users
- Reopening the interest selection dialog now pre-fills your previously saved tags

### 🐛 Mobile Fixes

- **Table horizontal scroll**: Wide tables in articles can now be swiped horizontally instead of wrapping character by character
- **Iframe blank areas**: When third-party embeds (videos, slides) are blocked by the browser, a "Open in new tab" fallback link now appears instead of a blank space
- **Player overlap**: Fixed bottom nav bar and mini player overlapping on mobile, content is no longer hidden behind the player
- **Header dropdown jump**: Opening the avatar dropdown no longer causes the page to shift horizontally
- **Horizontal scrollbar**: Fixed unexpected horizontal overflow on certain pages
- **"Not Interested" button wrapping on narrow screens**: Shows icon only on small screens, full text on wider screens
- **Complex layout articles**: Fixed rendering issues for articles from sites like OpenAI that use grid and column CSS layouts
