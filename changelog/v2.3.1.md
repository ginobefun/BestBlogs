## 中文

### 🔧 体验优化

- 新手引导整体更流畅，选源、提交、进入下一步都更跟手
- 换一批不再返回已经选中或已经看过的源，可以持续刷出新推荐
- 某个主题候选用完时按钮自动停用，并提示切换其他主题

---

## English

### 🔧 Improvements

- Onboarding feels more responsive overall — picking sources, confirming, and moving to the next step are all snappier.
- Show More no longer brings back sources you have already selected or seen, so refreshes keep surfacing new picks.
- When a category runs out of new picks, the button disables itself with a hint to try another topic.
