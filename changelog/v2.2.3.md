## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

- **Pro 早报全新卡片视图**：Pro 用户的每日早报从纯文本列表升级为三层差异化卡片——头条推荐（大卡配封面、核心观点、金句）、精选内容（中卡配评分和摘要）、补充阅读（紧凑列表），信息密度和阅读体验显著提升
- **独立推荐引擎**：Pro 早报不再依赖公共精选，而是基于你的关注源、兴趣标签和阅读历史，独立召回并个性化排序，确保每份早报都与你高度相关
### 🔧 体验优化

- **邮件三层渲染**：早报邮件同样采用分层设计——头条突出展示、精选内容附摘要、尾部快速浏览，在 Gmail 和 Outlook 中均能良好显示
- **早报导航栏**：新增顶部导航栏，可按类型筛选（文章/播客/视频/推文），也可快速跳转到头条、精选、补充三个区块
- **分享页同步升级**：Pro 早报分享页同步采用卡片视觉，查看者获得与 Pro 用户一致的阅读体验

### 🐛 修复

- 修复早报邮件中推文、播客、视频链接指向错误地址的问题
- 修复分享页内容展示与登录态不一致的问题
- 修复 Docker 健康检查在 Alpine 环境下因 IPv6 解析失败导致误报的问题

## English

### ✨ New Features

- **Pro Brief Card Redesign**: Pro daily briefs now feature a three-tier card layout — Top Picks (large cards with cover, key insights, and pull quotes), Featured (medium cards with scores and summaries), and More Reads (compact list) — significantly improving information density and reading experience
- **Dedicated Recommendation Engine**: Pro briefs no longer depend on public selections. Instead, they independently recall and rank content based on your followed sources, interest tags, and reading history, ensuring high relevance for every brief
### 🔧 Improvements

- **Three-Layer Email Rendering**: Brief emails adopt the same tiered design — headline highlights, featured content with summaries, and quick-scan tail section — rendering well in both Gmail and Outlook
- **Brief Navigation Bar**: A new top navigation bar lets you filter by content type (articles, podcasts, videos, tweets) and jump between Top Picks, Featured, and More Reads sections
- **Share Page Upgrade**: The Pro brief share page now uses the same card layout, giving viewers a reading experience consistent with Pro users

### 🐛 Fixes

- Fixed brief email links for tweets, podcasts, and videos pointing to incorrect pages
- Fixed share page content display being inconsistent with the logged-in view
- Fixed Docker health checks reporting false failures on Alpine due to IPv6 localhost resolution
