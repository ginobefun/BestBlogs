## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

- 世界杯特刊上线：可以按比赛查看赛前信息、赛后回顾、精选内容和赛事动态，适合看球前补背景，也适合睡醒后快速跟上办公室闲聊
- 普神 Agent 登场：普神会为每场比赛给出胜负预测和判断理由，让观赛更有代入感，你也可以做出自己的选择
- 我的早报偏好更细：可以选择来源语言类型，也可以开启仅从我的关注里推荐，让每天的个性化早报更贴近你的信息边界

## English

### ✨ New Features

- World Cup Special is live, with match previews, recaps, curated reading, and tournament updates, so you can catch up before kickoff or after waking up
- Prophet Agent joins the match: the Prophet predicts each result with a short rationale, making every game more fun to follow while leaving the final call to you
- More precise My Brief preferences: choose source language types and turn on followed-sources-only recommendations so your daily personal brief better matches your information boundary
