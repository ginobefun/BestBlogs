## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

**内建翻译（Pro 专属）**

文章页和推文详情页现在支持内建翻译，无需跳转 wenrun.ai 等外部工具。点击「翻译」按钮后，译文以段落为单位流式出现，与原文并排展示，便于对照阅读。

- **Pro 用户**：每天最多翻译 20 篇，译文自动缓存，二次点击秒速展示
- **免费用户**：可预览部分译文，升级 Pro 后解锁完整内容

### 🔧 体验优化

**订阅源管理：取消关注即时响应**

在「我的订阅源」页面点击取消关注后，该条目会立即从列表消失，页面不再重新加载，不再出现骨架屏闪烁，列表滚动位置也不会丢失。批量取消关注同样支持即时响应。

## English

### ✨ New Features

**Built-in Translation (Pro)**

Article pages and tweet detail pages now support inline translation — no need to jump to external tools like wenrun.ai. Click the "Translate" button and the translated text streams in paragraph by paragraph, displayed alongside the original for easy comparison.

- **Pro users**: Up to 20 translations per day; results are cached so repeat views load instantly
- **Free users**: Preview a portion of the translation; upgrade to Pro to unlock the full content

### 🔧 Improvements

**Subscription Manager: Instant Unsubscribe**

Clicking "Unsubscribe" in the "My Sources" page now removes the item immediately — no page reload, no skeleton screen flicker, and your scroll position is preserved. Bulk unsubscribe also responds instantly.
