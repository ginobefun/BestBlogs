## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

- Pro 即时早报：付费 / 升级 / 邀请生效后 15 分钟内即可在「我的早报」收到首份个性化早报，不再等到第二天清晨
- 全新首页：围绕你的私人阅读助手重新组织
- 添加更多源：粘贴 YouTube 频道、小宇宙节目、公众号文章链接，自动识别并接入 RSS
- 高分英文文章自动预翻译：高质量英文文章会提前准备好中文版本，打开即读

### 🔧 体验优化

- 我的空间四页合并为「我的阅读」单页：阅读历史、收藏、划线笔记统一入口，左侧筛选 + 右侧详情更顺手
- 我的关注与我的源合并为三列工作台：左侧分组、中间列表、右侧详情，移动和整理订阅源更直观
- 内容广场重设计：筛选条精简，「视角即视图」
- 精选周刊全新视觉：封面、评分、双语推荐语统一，移动端阅读体验更顺畅
- 新手引导优化：三步快速建立兴趣画像，离开还能继续，不会被弹窗反复打扰
- Pro 早报文案更亲切：会根据当前时段、工作日 / 周末变化问候语
- 我的回顾改为按钮触发：打开页面不再自动生成，避免误算资源
- 跨场景视觉打磨：色彩、间距、品牌主表达全局对齐，暗黑模式与移动端体验改善

## English

### ✨ New Features

- Instant Pro Brief: your first personalized brief arrives in My Brief within 15 minutes after Pro activation, upgrade, or invitation takes effect, no need to wait until the next morning
- Brand-new homepage: reorganized around your personal reading assistant
- Add more sources: paste a YouTube channel, Xiaoyuzhou podcast, or WeChat article link to auto-detect and add the RSS feed
- High-score English articles are pre-translated: top-quality English pieces get Chinese versions ready ahead of time, so you can read instantly

### 🔧 Improvements

- My Space merged into one My Reading page: history, bookmarks, and highlights in one place, with left filters and right detail for smoother browsing
- My Follows and My Sources merged into a three-column workspace: folders on the left, list in the middle, detail on the right, making it easier to move and organize feeds
- Explore redesigned: streamlined filter bar with view-is-view
- Newsletter visual refresh: unified covers, ratings, and bilingual recommendations, with smoother mobile reading
- Onboarding refined: three quick steps to build your interest profile, pause and resume anytime, without repeated popups
- Friendlier Pro brief copy: greetings adapt to the time of day and weekday vs weekend
- My Review now opens on demand: the page no longer auto-generates when you visit, avoiding wasted resources
- Cross-surface visual polish: colors, spacing, and brand voice aligned globally, with improved dark mode and mobile experience
