## English

### ✨ New Features

- Finish the three-step onboarding to claim a free 7-day Pro trial.
- Article, podcast, video, and tweet pages are now open to everyone, no sign-in needed to read.
- Redesigned Pro Center: this week's reading, AI usage, followed sources, and the last 7 daily briefs in one view.
- Invite friends: when a friend joins through your invite and finishes onboarding, both of you earn Pro days.
- Returning member rewards: long-time members who joined before May 15 can claim 14 days of Pro in one tap, once during the campaign.

### 🔧 Improvements

- Refreshed /pro page: editorial hero, Pro member quotes, and an early-bird countdown.
- Pro days from trials, payments, and other sources stack more reliably — subscribing early no longer drops what you already have.
- Free OpenAPI quota raised from 20 to 50 calls per day.
- Smoother page transitions; account info no longer re-fetches on every navigation under flaky networks.

### 🐛 Fixes

- Fixed the missing Manage Subscription button for some Creem-paid users in the Pro Center.
- Fixed iOS and Android in-app subscribers being mislabeled as gifted Pro users.
- Cancelling or refunding Pro no longer clears bonus days earned from trials or gifts.

> Friend invites and returning member rewards are now live. More ways to earn Pro days — including one-click promo claims from email campaigns — will roll out in upcoming releases. See [Pro Benefits & How to Earn](/docs/features/pro-benefits).
